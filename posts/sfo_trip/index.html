My adventures in San Francisco

Day 1:#

Flight to SF Got the window seat Amazing view of the mountains! Went to the hotel, refreshed & headed out Walked around, lot of weed smell, lots of walkers! Went to Salesforce Park - amazing view and plants Went to Pier 39 Sealions, sunset, Bueno Vista cafe with Irish coffee, Lombard street in the dark, Chinese Sizzling Pot King for dinner and back to hotel & sleep! Tv was atrocious at the hotel but the view was amazing

Day 2#

Onboarding! Went to chinatown in the evening but everything was mostly closed Got to see the fortune cookie place, had pineapple bun, went to Stella’s pasteries - awesome biscuits & treats, went to Golden Boy Pizza for a slice - so good! Got on the wrong direction bus hahaha, exchanged later on, went to hotel, got indian food & boba tea and slept!

Day 3#

Checkout & early morning onboarding 61st floor views were so good! Boarded the expensive bus to Palace Museum and went to the beach to view the Golden Gate Bridge Got the Waymo to hotel, dropped my phone while getting down, raised the complaint, left to San Jose in Bart Met friends, planned a trip! Knocked out with indian dinner

Day 4#

Pinnacle National Park - amazing park for beginner hikes! Easy hike with nice view & a cave route which was so cool to climb through. Good descent path and spotted a blue bird, some deer. Missed the concord eagles but apparently common there. Came back and collected my phone.

Day 5#

Wrapped up, goodbyes! Flight back to Seatac, saw Alien Romulus Developed a headache, so slept off for rest of day.

All in all, Bay Area is soooo cool!! <3
