Plantaincake

Reference: Plantain Cake Making Ingredients For serving size of 1 cake in 9x9 diameter cake 2 ripe plantains 2 cups flour 1 tsp Salt 1 Tsp baking powder 1/4 Tsp baking soda 3/4 cup sugar 8 tbsp butter or 1 stick butter 1/4 cup Yogurt 2 eggs 3 tbsp milk 1 tsp vanilla extract Recipe Preheat the oven to 350°F, then grease and flour the baking pan and set aside. Combine the flour, salt, and baking powder and baking soda. Whisk, mash or blend the plantains till fairly smooth. Use a hand whisk to cream the butter and sugar for 2 to 3 minutes. Add the eggs one at a time and cream together until just combined. Add the Yogurt and vanilla and whisk together. Mix the mashed plantains with the creamed butter and add the mixture to the dry ingredients (flour, salt, and baking powder). Fold a couple of times until well mixed. (don’t over mix). Stir in the milk and mix till combined. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan. Smooth out the surface of the batter and bake for 50 minutes to 1 hr or till a tester or toothpick inserted into the middle of the cake comes out clean. Let the cake cool down and then cut it and serve!

Sonarcloudlearning

Sonarcloud: Useful to get static code analysis. Configuration with Azure Pipelines: # Prepare SonarQube analysis configuration. - task: SonarQubePrepare@3 inputs: SonarQube: # string. scannerMode: 'MSBuild' cliProjectKey: # string. Required when scannerMode = CLI && configMode = manual. Project Key. projectKey: # string. Guidelines: Ensure to use version 3 and above. Previous ones are deprecated and will not be able to find the generated test analysis files. If the code and tests are under the same src folder, then write the files that need to be excluded in the .xml file: <ItemGroup> <TestProjects Include="src/Tests/**/*.csproj" /> </ItemGroup>
