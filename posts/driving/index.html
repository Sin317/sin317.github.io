My driving notes from training:#

On uphill#

Park and turn your wheel away from curb (towards left) if hard curb exists
In all other scenarios: turn wheel towards right

While driving out from the uphill park:#

Press brake hard Turn the steering Signal Move the gear to drive Then release the emergency brake Gently press the accelerator

While turning right look for pedestrians - turn as soon as right mirror crosses the curb While turning left go to center of road before turning the steering

Stop signs:#

Stop at white line, look for pedestrians Then move ahead to curb, if turning right check for oncoming traffic from left and front

At 4 way stop: Follow right of way Don’t hesitate - quick scan and keep moving

For changing lanes:#

Look at rear mirror Look at side mirror Look over shoulder

Mirror adjustment should be such that: W/o bending over to the side we should be able to still see the road If we bend we should be able to see part of car

While pulling over,#

Turn on indicator From the center of dashboard (end of the whiper on right) the curb should be visible then we are close enough Straighten the vehicle

When pulling out make the directions checks

To stay straight in lanes: keep the lane divider after the left speaker

To park -#

pulling in nosefront:#

Stop 1 line behind where we want to go and then turn and keep going

reverse park:#

Align with the space Turn left all the way and go 45 degree ahead Reverse - right all the way and back in and straighten

parallel park:#

  • Pull up just ahead of where we want to park (rear of car should match rear of front parked vehicle)
  • REVERSE
  • Right (till car makes a 45 degree) (trick: in the left mirror both the headlights of vehicle behind should be visible)
  • Straight (till left door handle just looks like its over the curb) (trick: the red line should just jump over the curb)
  • Left (till we are parallel with the drain line)
  • Park

NOTE: if we are more than 1 feet away from the car next to us, we have a wide angle so we will be closer to the front car. similarly for the other way round, we will have a narrow angle and be closer to the behind car.

Backing around on corner#

  • Left over shoulder, 360 degree check
  • Go back in a straight line
  • Just before the right turn, stop fully, scan again
  • Turn fully to the right till the car straightens out

Lane change#

  • Just 15 degree angle change on steering
  • Maintain speed

Shared lane#

  • Dont go in until we are <= 300 ft of turning
